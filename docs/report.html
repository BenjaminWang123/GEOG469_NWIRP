Confidentiality & Safety Notice

Information submitted through this platform will be kept confidential. It will not be shared with immigration enforcement, police, courts, or other legal agencies. Reports are collected only for Northwest Immigrant Rights Project research, advocacy, and community support purposes. For safety, please only provide approximate location information such as county, city, or general area. Do not submit a full street address or exact home address.

What To Do If You Encounter ICE Activity

  • Stay calm and do not run
  • Do not open the door unless there is a signed warrant
  • Document what you safely can
  • Report incidents to help protect communities
Learn More Safety Tips →

What Should You Report?

  • ICE sightings or presence
  • Raids, arrests, or detentions
  • Surveillance or suspicious activity
  • Traffic stops or checkpoints

Know Your Rights

  • You have the right to remain silent
  • You have the right to speak to a lawyer
  • You have the right to refuse consent to searches
  • You are protected by the Constitution
