What To Do If You Encounter ICE Activity

  • Stay calm and do not run
  • Do not open the door unless there is a signed warrant
  • Document what you safely can
  • Report incidents to help protect communities

What Should You Report?

  • ICE sightings or presence
  • Raids, arrests, or detentions
  • Surveillance or suspicious activity
  • Traffic stops or checkpoints

Know Your Rights
